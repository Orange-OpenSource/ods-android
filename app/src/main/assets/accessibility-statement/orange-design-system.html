Accessibility statement : Orange Design System

Context

Orange is committed to making its websites, mobile applications and digital street furniture accessible in accordance with Article 106 of Law No. 2016-1321 of October 7, 2016 for a Digital Republic, as described in its multi-year plan improvement of digital accessibility (PDF, 230Ko).

Conformity level:

"Orange Design System" is partially compliant to the criteria with WCAG version 2.2, level AA.

ORANGE SA refers to the WCAG international recommendations. Here is the correspondence link between the RGAA and the WCAG.

Feedback and contact information

If you are unable to access content or a service, you can contact the site manager to be directed to an accessible alternative or obtain the content in another form.

Legal remedies

You have reported to the website manager an accessibility problem that prevents you from accessing content or one of the portal's services and you have not received a satisfactory response, in this case:

  • Write a message to the Défenseurs des Droits.
  • Or contact the Défenseurs des Droits delegate in your department.
  • Or send a letter by post (free of charge, do not put a stamp)
